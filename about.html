About Collective Hair Systems

All of our stylists are qualified to design a new hair system or customize a stock unit, both human and synthetic. We are highly experienced cut-in specialists for new units as well as maintenance on current units, and any other hair need from cuts to color, or perms. All services are performed in individual cutting rooms allowing our guests to feel comfortable and enjoy a private experience.


Meet Our Stylists

Chris

Chris is a Seattle native and is the sole proprietor of Chris' Hair Systems since 1973 and has more than 50 years of styling experience.

He is well known in the hair replacement community as a knowledgeable, no nonsense artist. Chris believes in a 'no gimmick' approach to business creating an environment of a loyal clientele and staff.

Heidi

Heidi was born and raised in the Seattle area and has worked for Chris for over 10 years. She has a passion for hair coloring and specializes in color correction and perfecting individual looks for each of her clients.

Heidi is a versatile stylist and services anyone from age 2 to 100 for both hair replacement and standard service clients.

Her schedule is Tuesday through Friday 10 am to 5:30 pm and the last Saturday of every month 10 am to 5 pm. Call today to book an appointment 206-322-4747.

Laura

Laura has lived in Washington for over 18 years and has worked for Chris for over 8 years. Laura has over 29 years of hair dressing experience and excels at women's hair replacement but maintains a diverse clientele as well.

She offers expert knowledge and skill in many services from new cut-ins, to color and precision cuts.

Her schedule is Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday 9:30am to 2:30pm and Saturday 9am to 3:30pm. Call today to book an appointment 206-322-4747.
