About Us

Established in 1973, Chris' has drawn and maintained a loyal clientele due to the consistent, exceptional service received.

As a guest of our salon, you will feel at ease knowing you have experienced, skilled stylists perfecting your current style or customizing a polished new look to fit your personality.

Each of our operators have their own private room to ensure the utmost comfort level for you, our guest.
