Services

CHS offers precision haircuts, color, perms, hair relaxing and facial waxing.

CHS stylists are known for perfecting each cut to match their clients' lifestyle, hair type and face shape. At CHS we will start with a precise, personalized cut and finish by supplying you with the tools and knowledge to keep you looking 'salon fresh' every day.

Hair color is truly a science and at CHS we take color very seriously. Rest assured, whatever effect you desire, CHS' stylists can create it! From touch ups, to highlights or even color correction, we are color specialists.

We offer facial waxing and eyebrow and eyelash tinting comfortably in our private-service rooms.
